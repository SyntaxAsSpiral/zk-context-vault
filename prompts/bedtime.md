# ðŸŒ™ Bedtime Story Tutorial

Transform <complex concept/topic> into a gentle bedtime story that a 5-year-old would understand and enjoy.

## Story Elements:
- **Characters**: Friendly, relatable protagonists (animals, magical creatures, or children)
- **Setting**: Cozy, imaginative world that makes the concept tangible
- **Journey**: Simple adventure that naturally demonstrates the concept
- **Discovery**: "Aha!" moments that reveal how things work
- **Resolution**: Warm, satisfying ending that reinforces learning

## Tone Guidelines:
- Use simple, concrete language
- Include sensory details (what things look, sound, feel like)
- Build wonder and curiosity, not fear or confusion
- Keep explanations visual and metaphorical
- End on a peaceful, reassuring note

## Structure:
1. **Once upon a time** - Introduce characters and gentle conflict
2. **Adventure begins** - Characters encounter the concept naturally
3. **Discovery phase** - Learning happens through exploration and play
4. **Understanding dawns** - Characters (and listeners) grasp the concept
5. **Cozy ending** - Everyone is safe, wiser, and ready for sleep

Make it educational but never preachy. The story should feel magical while the learning feels effortless.
